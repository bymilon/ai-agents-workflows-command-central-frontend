# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be constructive, respectful, and welcoming. Participation should support collaboration, curiosity, and clear technical discussion.

Examples of behavior that help:

- Giving actionable, respectful feedback.
- Assuming good intent while still being honest.
- Sharing context when you disagree with an approach.
- Helping newer contributors get oriented.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or hostile tone.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior undermines a safe and productive community.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainer through [github.com/bymilon](https://github.com/bymilon).
