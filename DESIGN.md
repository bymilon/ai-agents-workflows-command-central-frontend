# Design System

Follows the guidelines for a Neo-enterprise darkglass aesthetic.

## Principles
1. **Command-centric**: Tactical, dense, low-noise interfaces.
2. **Keyboard-first**: Hotkeys for primary actions (simulated).
3. **Graph-native**: Infinite canvas for workflow operations.

## Colors
- **Surface**: Ultra-dark (mostly `#07080E`)
- **Borders**: Translucent white (`rgba(255,255,255,0.06)`)
- **Accents**: Semantic only. Purple (`#7A5AF8`) for primary interactions.

## Typography
- **Inter** for all UI.
- Monospace (`JetBrains Mono` or equivalent) for logs and expressions.

## Movement
- Max 180ms transitions. Opacity and transforms only. No bounces.
